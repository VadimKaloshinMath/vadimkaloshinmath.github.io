Klaudiusz's biography

Klaudiusz Czudek is a postdoc in Prof. Kaloshin group. He completed his PhD in 2021 in the Institute of Mathematics Polish Academy of Sciences under the supervision of Prof. Tomasz Szarek. His thesis was devoted to the ergodic and stochastic properties of random systems of interval homeomorphisms. Afterwards he spent a year as a postdoc in Nicolaus Copernicus University in Toruń in the group of Mariusz Lemańczyk and then received a Marie-Skłodowska fellowship in IST-BRIDGE program. Czudeks current research focuses on random dynamics on low dimensional manifolds and topics around. In the group he works mainly on random perturbation of Hamiltionian systems.
